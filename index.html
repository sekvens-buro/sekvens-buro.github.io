SEKVENS DJ FESTIVAL 30/8-1/9 2024

SEKVENS DJ FESTIVAL will be born on the 30st of August in the last summer days of the year 2024.
 
The FESTIVAL is within cycling distance from SVENDBORG (south of Funen, Denmark), and takes place among firs and deciduous trees on Aserbo's beautiful grounds, where there is room for adventure between the viking barns and midieval tower.

COCKTAILS – DANCEABLE DJs – BEACH – BEAUTIFUL PEOPLE – TENT PLACE IN SHADY FOREST - MEDIEVAL CASTLE TOWER DANCE FLOOR

 
SEKVENS DJ FESTIVAL is a small, exclusive festival!
Follow this page for continuous updates on the DJ line-up, as well as INFO about when ticket sales start.

ARTISTS:

Salti Brothers

Salti Brothers, Farid (DJ Salty) and Ahmed (Aggi), started DJing together after Ahmed returned from London, where he participated in founding the club FOLD and the record label DIY Records.

Salti Brothers specialize in acid, techno, house, electrobreaks and arab-disco.

soundcloud.com/ma-hada

Bilibili

BiliBili forkæler dansegulvets folk med en atmosfære af sexy og groovy prog trance og gammel tech house, der presser de +140 bpm. Efter at have pushet musik hele sit liv, har BiliBili fundet et specielt sted i sig for den nostalgiske halvfemser Goa-lyd og nuller UK-tech scene. Dog skal man regne med at blive overrasket med musik fra adskillige andre scener også - især den franske freetek- og Detroit electro scene. 

soundcloud.com/bilibilixx

pace+lead

pace+lead (he/him) is a sound designer by day and electronic experimentalist at night. He’s
been a part of the electronic music scene in Odense for years and is a resident DJ at
Odeology and Waterplz as well as co-founder of the latter.
pace+lead breathes experimentation and has a borderless approach to genres in his own
music. His latest EP-release "Escape" is a five track personal therapeutic flee into a universe
of contrasts. A euphoric address where fast and rolling grooves live side by side with weird
beats and dark pop with distorted vocals. The entanglement of the sonic entities is an
industrial landscape driven by emotional expression. This exploration will be further explored
in upcoming releases in 2024.
Alien sounds, liquidy atmospheres and sticky grooves should be expected to hit your
sensory canal system when he’s to be found behind the decks. The subgenres in his sets
can vary from tribal techno to trippy trance and hacker electro but hypnosis is always the
driving force of the pace+lead ufo.

soundcloud.com/paceandlead

Eline B

Eline B har i flere år produceret undergrundsarrangementer i Odense. Hun er kendt som initiativtageren til Hulen, Odeology og WATERPLZ. Eline ved, hvad et dansegulv har brug for. Hendes mixes bevæger sig mellem house, disco og techno, men på denne seneste sommernat dedikerer hun sig til techno.

mixcloud.com/ElineB89

Matriark

Copenhagen-based producer & DJ, Matriark has justifiably earned her
spot in the worldwide clubbing
scene. Her music productions and DJing energy reflect the 90s and 00s
trance era, but also expand the genre with sentimental value additions
of the current era. Being a part of the infamous Bunkerbauer
collective, a resident at Den Anden Side, one half of duo PLAZA,
co-founder of Megatech Industries and a DRIFT Kyiv resident, the
Norwegian artist is currently one of the most reliable trance
equipment creators around.

soundcloud.com/matriarkcph

Tec2nisk

Tec2nisk (ASTA MARI b2B Louie32)
Tec2nisk redefine techno with their track selection and genre fluidity. They distinguish themselves from the mainstream, taking listeners on a captivating and unpredictable journey through the depths of electronic music that is as brutal as it is mesmerizing. Prepare for a fusion of hardcore, acid, trance, breakbeat and electro.
 

soundcloud.com/louie32-0/tec2nisk-b2b-mix-1-louie32-asta-mari

Merkula

Merkula has played in Odense, Aarhus and Copenhagen. Making music for over 10 years, and 7 years of techno, she is planning to play some new unreleased tracks no one has heard before. Being a part of Odeology, it was only a given the answer was yes to play elsewhere on Funen. Her music as a mix of gritty heavy bangers to hypnotic evolving tracks.

 

soundcloud.com/merkula
