Matriark

Copenhagen-based producer & DJ, Matriark has justifiably earned her
spot in the worldwide clubbing
scene. Her music productions and DJing energy reflect the 90s and 00s
trance era, but also expand the genre with sentimental value additions
of the current era. Being a part of the infamous Bunkerbauer
collective, a resident at Den Anden Side, one half of duo PLAZA,
co-founder of Megatech Industries and a DRIFT Kyiv resident, the
Norwegian artist is currently one of the most reliable trance
equipment creators around.
