Matriark

Copenhagen-based producer & DJ, Matriark has justifiably earned her spot in the worldwide clubbing scene. Her music productions and DJing energy reflect the 90s and 00s trance era, but also expand the genre with sentimental value additions of the current era. Being a part of the infamous Bunkerbauer collective, a resident at Den Anden Side, one half of duo PLAZA, co-founder of Megatech Industries and a DRIFT Kyiv resident, the Norwegian artist is currently one of the most reliable trance equipment creators around. soundcloud.com/matriarkcph

pace+lead

pace+lead (he/him) is a sound designer by day and electronic experimentalist at night. He’s been a part of the electronic music scene in Odense for years and is a resident DJ at Odeology and Waterplz as well as co-founder of the latter. pace+lead breathes experimentation and has a borderless approach to genres in his own music. His latest EP-release "Escape" is a five track personal therapeutic flee into a universe of contrasts. A euphoric address where fast and rolling grooves live side by side with weird beats and dark pop with distorted vocals. The entanglement of the sonic entities is an industrial landscape driven by emotional expression. This exploration will be further explored in upcoming releases in 2024. Alien sounds, liquidy atmospheres and sticky grooves should be expected to hit your sensory canal system when he’s to be found behind the decks. The subgenres in his sets can vary from tribal techno to trippy trance and hacker electro but hypnosis is always the driving force of the pace+lead ufo. soundcloud.com/paceandlead
